# Alpvara

> [alpvara.janmueller.dev](https://alpvara.janmueller.dev).

Alpvara is a [Parqet](https://parqet.com) integration for Austria-specific features.
It does not save any data.
All data is kept in your Parqet account.

## Features

- The Watchtower highlights holdings that _could_ have taxation disadvantages in Austria, such as
  - REITs / BDCs,
  - non-reporting funds,
  - and Swap-based funds.
- The BaV import enables you to easily enter company pensions as a custom insurance in Parqet.
  - Since most insurance companies only provide yearly reports with yearly contributions and final balance, the monthly contributions and gains are approximated.
